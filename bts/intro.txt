## The Tools That Shaped the Vision

### 🛠 **Technologies and Frameworks**
Each project leverages cutting-edge tools to ensure scalability, performance, and user engagement. Some of the key technologies I’ve mastered include:

- **Frontend:** React.js, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide React
- **Backend:** Supabase, Firebase, Node.js
- **Cloud:** AWS, Google Drive API, Google Sheets Integration
- **Design:** Figma, shadcn-ui, Radix UI

### 🌐 **Collaboration & Deployment**
Collaboration and seamless deployment are integral to my work. With tools like Git, Vercel, and Netlify, I ensure every project reaches the audience in its most polished form.
